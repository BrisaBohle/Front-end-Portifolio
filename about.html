Brisa Bøhle

Front-end developer | Architect

Hello, my name is Brisa and I live in Trondheim. I am an educated architect with a passion for computers, and I urge to transition into working as a designer/developer, full time. I am from Brazil, there I completed a 5-year bachelor’s degree in Architecture and Urbanism, before moving to Norway. Then I enrolled in a 2-year master’s degree in Sustainable Architecture, at NTNU. After the Master I worked as an architect in Trondheim, before the “corona era” struck with all its consequences. And that is where my new story starts. I came across an interesting coding program, where I identified my interest and decided to begin my codding journey. I then enrolled in SheCodes program and quick enough began to fell on FLOW and enjoyment while coding. In less than a month I finished the workshops Basic and Plus. Designing this webpage in Responsive workshop. Front-end developer is my new path, and I can’t to see where it will leed me.
